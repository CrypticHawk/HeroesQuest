HEROES QUEST

You decide to follow the local. They stop on a side street off the one main road of town…
A hooded figure greets them, shaking their hands. The local hands the hooded figure a piece of paper and they part ways.
The local walks the opposite way up the street, while the hooded figure enters a large grey SUV which then pulls away. Your suspicions have been heightened…
You need to figure out what was on that paper.
