HEROES QUEST

The wind whips around your hair as you stand on the edge of the building,
the city below you alive with lights and the sounds of civilians going about their nights.
Horns beeping, people swearing & laughing, people hugging in greeting and others storming off.
A car pulls up to the alley way below you, several men and women dressed in black suits get out and walk through the large door.
The door closes behind them with a loud bang...
